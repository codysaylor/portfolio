While migrating roughly 200 properties away from a legacy CMS, I was instrumental in creating a new foundation on Drupal 8, including newly developed and WCAG accessible components.

Developing Reusable Components for OHIO

I have created a majority of the reusable components / elements used on OHIO's web CMS platform, Drupal. I accomplished this while working through other tasks such as bug fixes or website builds by communicating and collaborating with client and designers. During this work, I maintained brand standards, ensured accessibility, made elements versatile enough to be reused, and met client requirements.

Topic Previews: Topic previews are versatile components allowing users to optionally showcase an image, heading, descriptive text, and provide a link to another page.

Image Tiles: The image tile component offers a reusable and repeatable way for contributors to highlight photographs which represent content on their site.

Icon Tiles: Icon tiles provide visual interest to a group of links with the use of Font Awesome icons.

Explore Tabs: The explore more tabs element, initially created for the College of Fine Arts, offers a visually interesting way to direct attention.

The Heritage College of Osteopathic Medicine required additional changes to the image tiles component, allowing them to optionally add descriptive text.
