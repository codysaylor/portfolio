The performing arts website required a way to promote and showcase upcoming shows and events at Ohio University. I implemented a design which offers a detail list and a thumbnail / gallery view.

Roles & Responsibilities

Lead Developer: I developed the custom event browsing feature and facilitated the migration for Performing Arts.

Project Summary

The browse and filter component created specifically for performing arts was intended to highlight the event's cover art and any pertinent details about the event. I provided two layout options so users could use larger thumbnails or the detailed view if they preferred. There's also the option to filter by the start and end dates.
