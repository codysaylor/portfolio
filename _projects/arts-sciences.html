With over 3500 pages, the Ohio University College of Arts & Sciences site was one of the largest and most complex websites to migrate from into a new CMS and build.

Roles & Responsibilities

Project Manager: I managed the migration and development tasks and monitored staff resources. I also ensured any associated tasks by vendors were done on schedule.
Lead Developer: Working with a designer, I developed all new required features and facilitated the migration and build.

Project Summary

I handled the vast majority of this large-scale migration, including gathering requirements, client communication, delegation, implementation, and developing new elements. Some elements that I made include a dynamic weather interface, RSS feeds, and integration with 3rd party services such as Acalog.
