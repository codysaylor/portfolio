As part of the continuous Soul of Athens project at Ohio University, my team explored food insecurity in Appalachian Ohio through collaborative storytelling in an interactive website.

Project Details

Using an agile workflow, I worked with other designers, developers, and photo / video journalists to tell a cohesive story with an overarching theme in an.

The capstone class gave us the freedom to explore the different elements of creating a collaborative and larger-scale project that Untilled was. From wireframes to the final product, we went through different drafts, explored personas, and everything in between to get to the finished site and stories by the end of the semester.
